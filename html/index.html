About me


Greetings and welcome!


I’m Eric Walter Setälä ­ a 31-year-old student with a bold vision for the future. I’ve embarked on an exciting new journey as a first-year student at Haaga-Helia University of Applied Sciences, where my focus is on mastering Business IT and IT infrastructure. With this foundation, I’m set on becoming a key player in shaping the technological landscape of tomorrow.

A Proven Track Record


Before diving into the world of IT, I spent over 6.5 years working as a practical nurse in child welfare services at a foster home. This role shaped my ability to provide care in high-pressure environments, develop strong interpersonal skills, and lead teams with empathy and resilience. I then transitioned into the healthcare sector, working in domestic care services as part of a resource pool team for six months. Though these fields are quite different from IT, they taught me invaluable skills like problem-solving, adaptability, and the ability to understand complex human needs—qualities that I now apply to the challenges of business and IT.

Ready for New Challenges


After several years of dedication to others, I recognized my passion for technology and the need to push myself in a new direction. That’s why I chose to study at Haaga-Helia, where I’m now delving into the cutting-edge world of Business IT. I believe in the transformative power of technology and am excited about creating solutions that make businesses more efficient, innovative, and impactful.

Perspective Through Multilingual Skills


One of my strongest assets is my ability to communicate across cultures. Fluent in Finnish, English, and Russian, I am well-equipped to navigate international business environments. Whether working with global teams, understanding diverse markets, or building connections with clients from different cultural backgrounds, my multilingual skills give me a competitive edge.

Let’s Connect


With my unique blend of practical experience as a practical nurse, combined with my current studies in Business IT, I’m prepared for new challenges and opportunities. I am always excited to connect with professionals who share my passion for innovation and growth.
Feel free to take a look at my CV, and don’t hesitate to reach out if you'd like to explore potential collaborations. I’m looking forward to hearing from you!
